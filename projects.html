Projects

My projects reflect my interest in solving real-world problems using intelligent systems, automation, and modern web technologies. Each project is built with a focus on practical usability, logical design, and scalability.

Rather than creating simple demo applications, I aim to design systems that address actual challenges in domains such as retail automation, healthcare assistance, and environmental monitoring.

AI-Powered In-Store Shopping Assistant

This project focuses on transforming the traditional in-store shopping experience using artificial intelligence and automation.

Problem Statement

Large supermarkets often face challenges such as long queues, difficulty in locating products, inefficient stock handling, and poor customer experience during peak hours.

Solution Overview

The AI-powered shopping assistant is designed to receive customer grocery lists, check real-time product availability, estimate total bills, assist with item picking, and deliver items to a pickup point.

Key Highlights

  • Automated grocery list processing
  • Real-time stock validation
  • Intelligent bill estimation
  • Optimized in-store navigation support

Balanced Plate – AI Meal & Nutrition Assistant

Balanced Plate is an AI-powered application focused on promoting healthy eating habits through intelligent meal suggestions.

Problem Statement

Many individuals struggle to plan nutritious meals due to lack of knowledge, time constraints, and limited ingredient availability.

Solution Overview

The application suggests healthy meals based on user-provided ingredients, dietary preferences, and nutritional goals, rather than fixed dish names.

Key Highlights

  • Ingredient-based meal suggestions
  • AI-driven nutrition awareness
  • User-friendly and guided interaction
  • Health-focused design approach

Eco Watcher – Community Environmental Monitoring Platform

Eco Watcher is a digital platform designed to empower communities to report and monitor environmental issues collaboratively.

Problem Statement

Environmental pollution often goes unreported or unnoticed due to lack of accessible reporting tools and centralized monitoring systems.

Solution Overview

Eco Watcher enables users to report pollution incidents, visualize data on maps, and analyze environmental conditions at a community level.

Key Highlights

  • Community-driven reporting system
  • Environmental data visualization
  • Location-based monitoring
  • Awareness-focused platform design
