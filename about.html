About Me

I am Mohanarangan, a Computer Science and Engineering undergraduate currently pursuing my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) at C.P. Manakular Vinayagar Engineering College, Puducherry. I am presently in my second year of study and actively focusing on building strong technical and problem-solving skills.

I consider myself a highly motivated learner who is deeply interested in understanding how technology works at a system level. Rather than limiting myself to textbook knowledge, I focus on applying concepts through practical projects and real-world use cases.

Academic and Technical Identity

My academic journey in Computer Science has helped me develop a strong foundation in programming, logical reasoning, and structured problem solving. I continuously work on strengthening my understanding of core computer science concepts while exploring modern technologies.

I am particularly interested in areas where intelligence, automation, and efficiency intersect. I enjoy building systems that are not only functional but also scalable and user-centric.

Learning Philosophy

I strongly believe that true learning happens through implementation. Understanding theory is important, but applying concepts in real scenarios provides clarity and confidence.

Whenever I learn a new technology or concept, I make it a habit to:

  • Understand the core idea behind the concept
  • Explore real-world applications
  • Build small implementations or projects
  • Analyze limitations and improvement areas

This approach has helped me develop a practical mindset and the ability to learn independently.
